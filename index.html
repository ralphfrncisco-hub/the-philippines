Three Main Island Groups

The Philippines is an archipelago defined by its three great geographical divisions: Luzon to the north, Visayas at the center, and Mindanao in the south. Each group is an expansive world unto itself, offering vastly different experiences—from the metropolitan bustle and mountain retreats of Luzon to the historical islands and world-class beaches of Visayas, and the rich indigenous cultures and emerging adventure sports of Mindanao. Dividing your journey by these three pillars of the Philippines ensures you explore the full breadth of the nation's stunning geography and cultural diversity.
